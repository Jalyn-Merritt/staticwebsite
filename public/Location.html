SAVE Lab Location

Directions

The SAVE Lab is located in the North Paseo Building, Room 2.110

Go in through the main entrance of the North Paseo Building, then take the elevators to the 2nd floor. Take a left until you reach the end of the hallway, then take a right until you reach the end of the next hallway. 2.110 is at the end of that hall to your left.
